Home Labs, Virtualization & Self Hosting

Subject Level: Beginner → Advanced

This course teaches how to build, operate and maintain a personal computing infrastructure. Instead of relying entirely on third-party cloud services, you will learn how to run your own services, virtual machines and containers on hardware that you control.

By the end of this course, you will understand the architecture behind a modern homelab, deploy useful self-hosted applications, and build a platform that can later be extended with automation and a personal AI assistant.

Who is this Course For?

  • Developers who want a local server environment.
  • Students learning systems administration.
  • Privacy-conscious users who prefer self-hosting.
  • Media collectors building personal servers.
  • Home automation enthusiasts.
  • Anyone preparing to build a local AI infrastructure.

What You Will Learn

  • Homelab design principles.
  • Virtualization and containers.
  • Storage planning and NAS concepts.
  • Hosting personal services.
  • Networking fundamentals for homelabs.
