Apache Spark

1. Why Spark.

Data outgrew the single machine. It came in three waves:

Era Who makes the data
Early workers type it in — a few fields
Internet users generate it — forms, photos, videos
Now machines stream it — cars, watches, sensors, every second

Old way: bring the data to the processor. But now there's too much data — one processor drowns. New way: bring many processors to the data. That is parallel (distributed) processing, and Spark is the framework for it.

Think of a tollbooth on a freeway, ~1 minute per vehicle:

Setup 10 vehicles
1 booth (serial) 10 minutes
10 booths (parallel) 1 minute

No vehicle waits on another — split the work, combine at the end. Spark is a fast, in-memory, fault-tolerant engine that does exactly this over massive datasets.
