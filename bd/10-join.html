10. join.

The actions so far worked on one RDD; join brings together two — pairing two key–value RDDs on their key, bundling the matching values.

In code 
Python

x = sc.parallelize([("spark", 1), ("hadoop", 4)])
y = sc.parallelize([("spark", 2), ("hadoop", 5)])

x.join(y).collect()
# [('spark', (1, 2)), ('hadoop', (4, 5))]

The key stays once; the two values ride along as a pair.
