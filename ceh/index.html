Ethical hacking or Pentesting is the art of finding vulnerabilities in a live system and reporting the exploits to the respective organisation as part of the contract or as a participant in the bug bounty program hosted by the organisation (white-hat), for monetary or reputation purposes. The second last part matters a lot. If you find vulnerabilities and don't report, it's a grey moral zone. Adding to this, if you find bugs outside of the scope described in bug bounty program, it won't be fruitful, for you. Usually, withholding such information is individual's loss, as organisations do pay handsomely if the hole and proposed fix is legit. However, if you disclose the discovery to malicious third party or act maliciously (black-hat) using the obtained information, you're eligible to be in jail. White-hats turning on their employer for personal vendetta are called grey-hats. These guys are worse than black-hats, atleast they're honest.

On the other hand, large organisations often operate this process of pentesting and defence using various teams.
Types of teams:

  • Red team - offense. Team responsible for finding holes and reporting to Blue team.
  • Blue team - defence. Team responsible for fixing these holes as reported by Red team.
  • Purple team - all rounders/hybrid. Team responsible assisting both teams for immediate fixes.

Types of participants:

  • Black hats - Criminals. E.g., Kevin Mitnick.
    Common activities
    • Ransomware
    • Phishing
    • Credential theft
    • Malware deployment
    • Selling stolen databases
  • White hats - Professionals. E.g., Bug hunters, Red team, Consultant.
  • Grey hats - White hats turned Black. E.g., Disgruntled employee, Mercenaries.
  • State-Sponsored / APT Groups. E.g. Lazarus Group.
    • Common activities
    • Disruption of enemy's national infrastructure
    • Spying, and Surveillance
    • Selling stolen databases
  • Red hats - Vigilantes.
  • Hackitivists - Individuals bent on exposing scandals and thwarting dictatorship. E.g., Anonymous.
    • Common activities
    • DDoS attacks
    • Leaks
    • Protest campaigns
  • Script Kiddie / Green hat - Beginners/ Amatuers - Us.
