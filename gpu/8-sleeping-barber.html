Canonical problems

8. Sleeping barber.

Like producer–consumer, but order does not matter. A finite waiting room; the barber serves one customer at a time.

Situation Failure
room full, customers keep arriving they see it full and leave → over-utilization
no customers barber sits idle → under-utilization

Solution
Idea Effect
Invert the problem let the number of waiting customers decide the number of barbers
