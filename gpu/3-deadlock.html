Pitfalls of shared state

Difficulty: Medium

3. Deadlock.

Each thread holds a resource and needs another that someone else is holding. Nobody releases. Everyone waits forever.

Thread Holds Waiting for
A Lock 1 Lock 2
B Lock 2 Lock 1

A circular wait. The naive fix — "everyone drop your locks" — just becomes a grab-frenzy that may not help.


Solution
Rule Effect
Fixed lock order Everyone takes Lock 1 before Lock 2 → no cycle
All-or-nothing Acquire every needed lock at once, or none
