Concurrency patterns

Most parallel solutions fit one of five patterns. Recognize the pattern and skip reinventing the wheel.

10. Divide and conquer.

Split the data until each piece is small enough to answer, then combine the answers on the way back up. Binary search and merge sort work this way.

Good Bad
less total work on sorted data split/merge overhead on unsorted data
natural fit for recursion poor on GPUs / CUDA (recursion is costly there)
