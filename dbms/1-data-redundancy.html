Demerits of file-based system

File-based systems became difficult to manage as data volume, complexity, and concurrent users increased. The following challenges in file-based system were noted, and consequently DBMS was introduced solving them.

1. Data redundancy.

The same information being kept in several different places, as different individuals may required to keep copy of certain data.

Customers.dat
CustID CustomerName City Phone
