Safety & Permissions

Difficulty: Intermediate

A capable AI assistant should not have unrestricted access to your computer. The same tools that make it useful can also make it dangerous if used without limits. Safety is therefore a system design requirement rather than an optional feature.

Principle of Least Privilege

Grant the assistant only the permissions required for its current task.

Recovering From Mistakes

  • Version important files.
  • Keep reliable backups.
  • Support undo operations where possible.
  • Test automation before broad deployment.

Key Takeaway

Trustworthy assistants are built around clear permissions, explicit approvals and complete transparency. The objective is not maximum automation, but reliable automation that keeps the user in control of important decisions.
