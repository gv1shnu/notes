Computer Vision

Difficulty: Intermediate

Until now, our assistant has primarily understood text. However, much of our digital life consists of images: screenshots, scanned documents, diagrams, photographs and user interfaces. Computer vision enables an AI assistant to interpret visual information and integrate it into the same knowledge system used for text.

What Is Computer Vision?

Computer vision is the field of artificial intelligence concerned with extracting useful information from images and video.

Privacy Considerations

  • Process sensitive images locally whenever practical.
  • Review extracted information before sharing it.
  • Protect personal photographs and scanned documents.
  • Limit access to image archives.

Key Takeaway

Computer vision extends a personal AI assistant beyond text. By understanding documents, screenshots, photographs and interfaces, the assistant can build a richer knowledge base and interact with visual information using the same natural language workflows introduced earlier in this course.
