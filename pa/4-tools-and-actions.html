Tools & Actions

Difficulty: Intermediate

A language model can reason, explain and plan, but it cannot directly interact with your computer. To become a useful assistant it must be connected to tools that perform actions such as searching files, launching applications or checking the status of your homelab.

Reasoning vs Acting

Language ModelTool
Explains how to rename files.Renames approved files.
Suggests a backup strategy.Starts a backup task.
Identifies a storage issue.Reads disk usage statistics.
Plans a workflow.Executes the workflow.

Key Takeaway

A personal AI assistant becomes useful when it combines reasoning with controlled interaction. The language model plans and interprets, while tools perform actions in a safe, auditable manner.
