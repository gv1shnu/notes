AI-Powered Personal Computing

Subject Level: Intermediate → Advanced

Artificial Intelligence is rapidly becoming part of everyday computing. Rather than using an AI only as a chatbot, this course explores how to build a personal AI assistant that understands your computer, automates repetitive work and helps manage your digital life.

Unlike cloud-based assistants, the emphasis is on running AI locally whenever practical, integrating it with your own infrastructure and keeping you in control of your data, automation and workflows.

Prerequisites

  • Digital Privacy
  • Data Hoarding
  • Home Labs, Virtualization & Self Hosting

These subjects provide the foundation for building an AI that can safely interact with files, applications, services and infrastructure.
