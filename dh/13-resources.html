A well-maintained archive becomes more valuable with time. Continue refining your organization, documenting your collections and periodically reviewing storage media, backup procedures and integrity verification methods.

Final Thoughts

Data hoarding is not measured by the number of terabytes you own. It is measured by how effectively you can locate, understand, verify and preserve the information that matters to you. A disciplined archive remains useful decades after it is created because it is organized, documented and maintained with intention.
