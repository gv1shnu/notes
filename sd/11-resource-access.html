The structure: The Method

11. Resource access.

ResourceAccess hides how a resource is reached. The classic mistake is to expose CRUD on its interface — insert/select betrays a database, open/read/seek betrays a file. Swap the store and everyone upstairs detonates.

Instead, expose atomic business verbs: verbs so fundamental they can't be expressed with other verbs.

Verb Atomic?
transfer money no — it's credit + debit
credit / debit an account yes

Atomic verbs pertain to the nature of the business, so they never change — banks have done credit/debit since the Medici. Internally, ResourceAccess transforms the verb into actual store calls, so you can swap database → file → cache without touching anyone above it.
